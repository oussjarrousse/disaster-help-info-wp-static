Organisations accepting donations with focus on Turkey<\/h3>\n\n\n\n
\n
AKUT<\/strong>
AKUT is entirely a voluntary, non-governmental organization involved in searching, assisting and rescuing all who require aid within its authority and means, in mountain or other nature-related accidents, natural disasters and all other emergency conditions by means of trained, disciplined, high-standard personnel and equipment, passing on knowledge to society with no political affiliation<\/a><\/div>\n\n\n\n
AHBAP<\/strong>
AHBAP<\/strong> is a non-governmental organisation founded by the Turkish musician and philanthropist Haluk Levent, based on solidarity and cooperation. AHBAP works in areas such as social aid, animal rights, humanitarian aid, natural disaster coordination and assistance<\/a><\/div>\n\n\n\n
AFAD<\/strong>
The Disaster and Emergency Management Authority (AFAD) is the Turkish governmental organization, that was established after Turkish Parliament passed Law No.5902 in 2009. AFAD currently has 81 provincial branches across Turkey in addition to 11 search and rescue units.<\/a><\/div>\n<\/div>\n\n\n\n
